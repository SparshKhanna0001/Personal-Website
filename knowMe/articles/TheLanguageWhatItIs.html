The Language,What It Is?

Language, according to me is an event describing tool.
For example; I ate an apple Here, the event is me eating the apple. So, when i will say this sentence, the listener will picturize me eating an apple in his mind ,and hence the event of eating an apple is described appropriately.


Why do we need language?

We need a language because it is so far the most efficient tool to describe something. Let's say instead of telling
